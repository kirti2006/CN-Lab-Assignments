EXPERIMENT 1 – BASIC NETWORK TOPOLOGIES

-----------------------------------
TASK 1: STAR TOPOLOGY
Ping from PC0 to PC1 – Successful
Ping from PC0 to PC2 – Successful
Ping from PC0 to PC3 – Successful

Observation:
All devices communicated through switch successfully.
Switch is single point of failure.

-----------------------------------
TASK 2: HUB TOPOLOGY
Ping successful.

Observation:
Hub broadcasts data to all ports.
More collisions observed.
Lower performance than switch.

-----------------------------------
TASK 3: RING-LIKE TOPOLOGY
Ping from PC0 to PC3 – Successful.

Observation:
Switches connected in loop.
STP blocked one link.
Alternate path available.

-----------------------------------
TASK 4: FAILURE TEST
After disconnecting one link, ping still successful.

Observation:
Network recovered using alternate path.
Better fault tolerance than star topology.
